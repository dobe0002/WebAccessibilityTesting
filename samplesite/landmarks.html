Landmarks

Things to check for:

  • Main content is surrounded by a "main" landmark. - There should only be 1 main landmark.
  • Banners are wrapped in a "header" landmark
  • Navigation elements are wrapped in a "navigation" landmark
  • Footers are wrapped in a "contentinfo" or "footer" landmark.
  • How to check (new window)
